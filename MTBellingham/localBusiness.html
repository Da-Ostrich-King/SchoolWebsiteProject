Things to Do in Bellingham While You Are Here

Places to Eat Before and After a Ride

  • Lafeens Donut Shop

    • Lafeens Donut Shop is a donut shop on Lakeway Road right next to were you enter and exit the north side Galbraith Mountain. They have amazing donuts and drinks and is a great place to get your sugar fix before a ride.

  • Davinci's Market

    • Davinci's Market was opened in September of 2000 by Kevin and Kim Weatherill. With over 50 years of independent food retailing experience we knew that our business could be a lot better than the typical c-store franchise programs you find on every corner. Everyday we are working to build a fun shopping and high food quality experience for our neighborhood friends.

    Davinci's Market is right next door to Lafeens Donut Shop so you can follow the same map for Lafeen's to the north side trails.
